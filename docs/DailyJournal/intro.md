# Daily Journal

This section is the public-safe running log for ongoing engineering work.

It exists to make real systems work more visible without turning the docs site into noise.

## What belongs here

- concise daily build notes
- architecture and debugging summaries
- rollout notes from active engineering work
- public-safe writeups extracted from private or internal projects

## What does not belong here

- secrets or private infrastructure details
- client-confidential implementation specifics
- filler activity with no technical signal

## Working rule

Each entry should explain:

1. what was worked on
2. what technically changed or was learned
3. why it matters
4. what happens next

The goal is not fake activity. The goal is durable public proof of real engineering output.
